ZAN CSO Database: Global Fund Support

This Civil Society Database was developed by the Zimbabwe AIDS Network (ZAN) with support from the Global Fund.

ZAN: Uniting Zimbabwe's CSOs for Health

The Zimbabwe AIDS Network (ZAN) serves as the coordinating body for Civil Society Organizations (CSOs) in Zimbabwe, with the mandate to unify and support their efforts in addressing the HIV and AIDS pandemic, as well as other non-communicable diseases and emerging health challenges.

CSO Database: Collaboration & Partnership

The Zimbabwe CSO Database is a valuable resource for stakeholders, including government agencies, donors, and development partners, seeking to collaborate with Civil Society Organizations.

Find Local CSOs: Community Benefits

Communities and individuals can benefit by using the database to locate CSOs providing essential services in their areas.

Database: Aligning CSOs with SDGs

The database plays a crucial role in aligning the efforts of CSOs with national health priorities and the Sustainable Development Goals.

Verify NGOs: RDC & Government Use

Rural District Councils, government ministries, and departments can utilize the database to verify the authenticity of NGOs operating within their jurisdictions, ensuring transparency and accountability.
